What is Cataract?

A cataract is a natural clouding of the eye’s lens, causing blurred vision and difficulty seeing clearly. It usually develops with age but can also occur due to diabetes, eye injuries, long-term medication use, or hereditary factors. At Ushodaya Laser Eye Hospital, we diagnose and treat cataracts using the latest, safest, and most advanced laser-assisted techniques.

Common Symptoms of Cataract

  • ✔ Blurred or cloudy vision
  • ✔ Difficulty seeing at night
  • ✔ Sensitivity to bright lights or glare
  • ✔ Halos around lights
  • ✔ Colors appearing faded
  • ✔ Frequent change in glasses power
  • ✔ Trouble reading, driving, or watching TV

Based on your lifestyle and vision needs, we offer different lens options:

  • ✔ Toric Lens: Best for patients with astigmatism

When Do You Need Cataract Surgery?

Cataract surgery is recommended when the cloudy lens starts interfering with daily activities like reading, driving, working, or recognizing faces. If your vision does not improve with glasses, surgery becomes the only effective solution.

Recovery After Cataract Surgery

Cataract surgery is a quick and painless procedure. Most patients notice clearer vision within a few hours. Full healing usually takes 2–4 weeks, during which you will receive medications and follow-up care from our specialists. You can resume daily activities within 24–48 hours.
