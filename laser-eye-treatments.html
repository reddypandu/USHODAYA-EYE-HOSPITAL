Laser Eye Treatments

Advanced, Safe & Highly Precise Laser Vision Correction

At USHODAYA LASER EYE HOSPITAL, we offer world-class laser eye treatments using the most advanced technology to correct vision problems. Whether you have nearsightedness, farsightedness, astigmatism, or age-related issues, our laser vision correction procedures help you achieve clearer, sharper, and glasses-free vision.

Our highly experienced surgeons use cutting-edge laser systems to reshape the cornea with unmatched accuracy. Every treatment plan is customized based on corneal thickness, eye power, lifestyle needs, and overall eye health—ensuring the best and safest outcomes for every patient.

LASIK Laser Surgery

A highly effective laser treatment to correct myopia, hyperopia, and astigmatism. Provides fast recovery with minimal discomfort and long-lasting clear vision.

Bladeless Femto LASIK

A 100% blade-free, flap-based laser procedure using femtosecond technology for maximum precision, comfort, and safety.

SMILE Laser Surgery

A minimally invasive, flapless laser vision correction method ideal for active individuals. Faster healing and less dry eye compared to LASIK.

PRK / Surface Ablation

Recommended for patients with thin corneas. A surface-based laser treatment that provides excellent vision correction without creating a corneal flap.

Contoura Vision Laser

A topography-guided customized LASIK treatment that provides HD vision and improves corneal irregularities for superior clarity.

Laser Treatment for Diabetic Retinopathy

Specialized retinal laser to stop bleeding, prevent swelling, and protect vision in diabetic patients with retina damage.

What Are Laser Eye Treatments?

Laser eye treatments are advanced procedures that reshape the cornea using precision laser technology to correct refractive errors. These treatments eliminate or reduce the need for glasses or contact lenses while enhancing clarity and quality of vision.

Benefits of Laser Eye Treatments

  • ✔ Quick, safe & painless procedures
  • ✔ Permanent correction for eye power
  • ✔ Fast recovery with minimal downtime
  • ✔ Blade-free & flapless options available
  • ✔ Suitable for people with active lifestyles
  • ✔ Long-term freedom from glasses & lenses

Who Is Eligible for Laser Vision Correction?

Most people between **18 and 45 years** can undergo laser treatment if they meet these criteria:

  • ✔ Stable eye power for at least 1 year
  • ✔ No active eye infections or diseases
  • ✔ Adequate corneal thickness
  • ✔ Not pregnant or breastfeeding

Recovery After Laser Eye Treatment

Most patients notice a significant improvement in vision within 24–48 hours. You may experience mild dryness or irritation for a few days. Full recovery typically occurs within 1–2 weeks depending on the laser procedure.
